Game Review

Pico Park shines as a multiplayer experience focused on teamwork and communication. Players must coordinate movements, share responsibilities, and solve puzzles together, often leading to chaotic and hilarious moments. The simplicity of controls makes it accessible, but the increasing difficulty keeps players engaged. While it may feel short, the fun comes from playing with others.
