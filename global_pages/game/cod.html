Call of Duty: Black Ops III is a futuristic military shooter set in 2065, where cybernetic enhancements and robotic warfare have redefined the battlefield. It is known for its momentum-based movement system, allowing fluid wall-running, sliding, and thrust-jumping. The game features a mind-bending co-op campaign, a highly acclaimed Zombies mode, and a multiplayer that introduced Specialists with unique abilities.

Game Review

Black Ops III offers few surprises, but even so, it's a great package. The campaign sometimes feels like it's trying too hard, but is ultimately solid and enjoyable. Zombies mode is a nice bonus. But really, it's the multiplayer that steals the show. It doesn't stray far from the usual formula, but it's been honed and polished to perfection to deliver seriously brilliant multiplayer competition.
