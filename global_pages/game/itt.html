Action Co-op Adventure Platformer Funny

It Takes Two is a genre-bending, co-op only platform adventure developed by Hazelight Studios. The game follows the clashing couple Cody and May, who are magically transformed into dolls and trapped in a fantastical world. To mend their fractured relationship and return home, they must navigate a whimsical, ever-changing world where every level introduces a brand new gameplay mechanic.

Game Review

It Takes Two is a spectacular co-op adventure that consistently innovates and charms, offering a truly unforgettable experience. The game offers flawless pacing in both story and gameplay, layering new mechanics and challenges into a series of unforgettable set-pieces. The narrative, which mirrors Cody and May's dysfunctional relationship, is told with Pixar-worthy heart and humor, without ever feeling preachy. With its brilliantly designed cooperative puzzles, impressive variety, and polished feel, It Takes Two is not just an excellent game, but a landmark title that proves co-op gaming is an art form.
